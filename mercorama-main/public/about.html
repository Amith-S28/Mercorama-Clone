Our Story

From Global Experience to Global Impact.

Mercorama was founded by a team with deep experience in international trade, market development, and business growth.

We saw firsthand how challenging it is for businesses to access the right intelligence and expertise to expand globally.

So we built Mercorama—a platform that combines AI-powered trade intelligence with a global network of experts to help businesses move forward with clarity and confidence.
