The Challenge

Global growth is hard.
You don't have to do it alone.

Canada has over 1.1 million SMEs with global growth potential.
Yet around 72% of Canadian exports still go to the U.S.

Canada already has access to 50+ global markets through free trade agreements like CETA and CPTPP.
But less than 30% of SMEs fully use those benefits.

The problem is not lack of information.
It is that trade information is scattered, technical, and hard to act on.
