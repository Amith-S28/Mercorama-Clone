About
Professional Summary

Mohamed Anwar is a senior international trade advisor and export market expansion strategist with over 23 years of experience across the private sector and international development. Based in Cairo, Egypt, he specialises in helping businesses — particularly SMEs — access new markets, grow exports, and build durable B2B relationships across the EU, MENA, and U.S. markets.

His career spans export management, logistics and procurement operations, and high-impact advisory roles. Most recently, Mohamed served as Senior Export Advisor at USAID TRADE (Trade Reform & Development in Egypt), where he led export growth strategies for Egyptian SMEs in home textiles and readymade garments — enabling over $20 million in confirmed export contracts through buyer matchmaking and strategic market intervention.

Mohamed brings trilingual capability (Arabic, English, French) and deep operational experience across international trade fairs, buyer missions, export documentation, compliance, and deal negotiation — making him uniquely equipped to bridge African, Middle Eastern, and global markets.
