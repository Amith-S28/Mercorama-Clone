About
Professional Summary

Toufik Amrani is a bilingual (English, French, and Arabic) international trade and maritime operations specialist with deep expertise in dry bulk chartering, laytime calculations, Incoterms® 2020, and international trade documentation. He holds the CITP|FIBP® designation — Canada's gold standard for certified international trade professionals.

Over the past decade, Toufik has managed and negotiated complex chartering operations, supported grain producers and commodity traders, and helped businesses in import/export across bulk cargo sectors optimize their cost structures, reduce demurrage exposure, and improve operational efficiency.

  • Dry bulk chartering structure and freight negotiation

  • International trade documentation and customs compliance
  • Shipment planning and logistics cost optimization
  • Agri-commodity and grain export planning
  • Seafood and perishable cargo logistics
  • Trade finance fundamentals and supply chain risk management
